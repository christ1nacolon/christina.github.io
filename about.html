Christina Colón
I am a student at Indiana University Bloomington majoring in Informatics with a specialization in Business and a minor in Spanish. My graduation date is May 2022. I am originally from the Chicagoland area and can definetly consider myself a "Midwesterner" at heart. Throughout my time in undergrad I have taken up a plethora of wonderful opportunities. I have traveled across the country to present research and attend conferences, and even left the states for the first time during my study abroad. I have presented my research in Informatics at Harvard University, Indiana University, and Indiana University-Purdue University Indianapolis. Currently I work as an Undergraduate Researcher in the ProHealth Informatics lab and also hold a Senior Peer Consultant Position within FASE Mentoring- a division of OVPDEMA(Indiana University's diversity office). As an SPC I mentor incoming freshmen, probationary students, and other mentors.
